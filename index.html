About Our Hotel

Comfort & Convenience in Zirakpur

At Hotel SK Merritts, we focus on providing a relaxed, comfortable stay with outstanding service and great value. Situated prominently on the Ambala–Chandigarh Highway, our hotel is the perfect gateway for both business travelers and leisure guests visiting Chandigarh, Zirakpur, or transit points nearby.

We offer modern accommodations, attentive customer care, and excellent local accessibility, ensuring your journey is seamless and stress-free.
